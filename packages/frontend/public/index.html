What Does This Mean?

What is a Blockchain?

Think of it as a public ledger that records all transactions. Every transaction is grouped into blocks, creating a chain of records that can't be altered.

Why is Solana Fast?

Solana can process thousands of transactions per second, making it one of the fastest blockchains. This speed enables real-time applications and low fees.

How is it Secure?

Hundreds of validators around the world verify every transaction. This decentralized network ensures no single entity can control or manipulate the blockchain.

What are Tokens?

Tokens are digital assets on the blockchain. SOL is Solana's native token used for transactions, while other tokens represent various projects and currencies.
